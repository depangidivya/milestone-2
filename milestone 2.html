Dr APJ Abdul Kalam

Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist who served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007 and was born on October 15,1931. Dr APJ Abdul Kalam is known as the Missile man of India due to his contributions to the development of ballistic missile and launch vehicle technology.

Abdul Kalam received numerous prizes,including the Bharat Ratna,Padma Bhushan.He is a great inspiration and inspired many people with his messages and quotes.As a politician,he played a leading role in the development of India.He passed away due to a cardiac arrest on July 27,2015.He,however continues to live in the hearts of every Indian.
